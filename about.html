About Kenneth Frieson

Ken Frieson is a piano player from Garnet Valley, PA. Growing up in Philadelphia, PA he learned to love music at an early age as he started taking classical piano lessons at the age of four. Ken has studied music at West Chester University and Eastern University and is very passionate about sharing his love for music. He has taught kindergarten through eighth grade music and directed the school choir at Antonia Pantoja Charter School in addition to teaching private piano lessons for 6 years at Gibsonâ€™s School of Music and Arts.

Currently serving as Worship Pastor at Master Builders Church, he plays a variety of Christian music ranging from contemporary to gospel styles. During his time there Ken has released two instrumental albums; Devotion and Defending The Sacrifice, and is currently working on another album being released this year.
